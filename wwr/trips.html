Turtle Soup

Embark on an unforgettable adventure with our "Turtle Soup" rafting trip! Perfect for beginners and families, this scenic route winds through gentle rapids and tranquil waters, offering a relaxing yet exhilarating experience. Paddle through lush forests and spot local wildlife, including playful turtles sunbathing on the riverbanks. Our experienced guides ensure a safe and enjoyable journey for all ages. Whether you're looking to unwind in nature or share an exciting day with loved ones, Turtle Soup promises a delightful escape into the great outdoors. Join us for a memorable day on the water!

Paradise

Experience the ultimate thrill with our "Paradise" rafting trip! Known for its breathtaking views, this journey begins with serene, picturesque landscapes that will leave you in awe. But don't let the tranquility fool you—once you're on the raft, the adventure kicks into high gear! Navigate through challenging rapids and feel the adrenaline surge as you conquer each twist and turn. This trip is perfect for thrill-seekers looking to combine the beauty of nature with the excitement of extreme rafting. Our expert guides are there to ensure your safety and maximize your fun. Come join us for an unforgettable ride in Paradise!

Hunger Dragon

Prepare yourself for the ultimate rafting challenge with our "Hunger Dragon" trip, the most extreme adventure we offer. Designed exclusively for adults, this two-day expedition begins with a thrilling helicopter ride to a remote and untouched wilderness. Once you arrive, the real adventure begins. Tackle some of the wildest and most intense rapids, navigating through powerful currents and steep drops that will test even the most experienced rafters. With roaring waters and heart-pounding excitement, Hunger Dragon promises an unparalleled experience for true adrenaline junkies. Join us for a once-in-a-lifetime adventure that will push your limits and leave you craving more!
